# Prompt Patterns

Author: [Shinning](https://shinning.pro/)

Use this reference when the prompt needs stronger photographic detail.

## Positive Prompt Structure

A strong photo prompt usually follows this order:

1. Main subject and identity/reference constraints
2. Scene, location, and time of day
3. Subject pose, expression, action, wardrobe, or product placement
4. Composition, framing, camera angle, and perspective
5. Lens, device feel, depth of field, and camera treatment
6. Lighting source, direction, contrast, and shadow quality
7. Color palette, grading, texture, and mood
8. Realism and quality constraints

## Realism Details

Use realism constraints when the output should feel like a real photo:

- realistic skin texture
- natural facial expression
- accurate human anatomy
- believable hands and fingers
- natural body proportions
- physically plausible lighting
- clean but lived-in environment details
- no over-polished beauty retouching
- no plastic skin
